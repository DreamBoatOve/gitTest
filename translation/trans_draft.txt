The digital twin system for engineering materials performance evaluation
-------------------------------------------------
For the development of the digital twin system for marine engineering materials performance evaluation, we need to reveal more than service performance of multi-field and multi-factor coupling environment engineering materials across scales correlation and the law of the evolution is, solve the problem of which the core of the scientific, namely material service performance evaluation of project "the scales" and "environmental domain" and "time domain" scientific problems and the coupling effect between the three.
-------------------------------------------------
本项目所提出的“工程材料服役性能数字孪生评价系统”是通过多尺度、多场耦合环境和多种失效形式的实验研究，积累海量服役性能数据，提炼物理模型，并结合数据挖掘和人工智能技术建立失效动力学仿真数字化模型，构建实际工程材料和装备的虚拟空间映射，借助其与现场实时监检测数据的多重迭代，优化仿真预测模型，实现对工程材料和装备服役性能的精准评价、预测与三维可视化
